About Me

I believe that therapy is a collaborative journey. My approach is rooted in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), but I integrate mindfulness and trauma-informed practices to tailor our sessions to your unique needs.

Throughout my twelve years of experience, I have worked with diverse populations in both Algeria and Europe, helping clients navigate the complexities of identity, modern stress, and deep-seated emotional hurdles. Our sessions are a safe, non-judgmental harbor where your voice is the most important element.
